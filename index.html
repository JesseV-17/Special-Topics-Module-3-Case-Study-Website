Introduction

This project represents the final expansion of our workout-inspired smart mirror. Building on the insights and developments from the previous two projects, we focused on enhancing both the interface and the physical design of the mirror.

During development, we explored disassembling the Christie Cubes but encountered significant technical challenges that made this approach impractical within the project timeline. To address this, we collaborated with the Mac tech team and decided to integrate a TV monitor instead. We measured the monitor and its stand, and designed a custom frame to accommodate it, ensuring stability and proper functionality.

The resulting prototype successfully demonstrates the concept of a hover based smart mirror tailored for gym environments, combining our refined interface design and a two way mirror frame.

Module 2: Prototype

Research

This project builds on the work completed in Project 1. In this phase, Jesse and I continued developing the concept of a gesture based smart mirror for fitness environments, with a focus on prototyping the system and exploring how it could be realistically implemented.

Our research centred on the technical components required to build the mirror, including the software used to power the interactive display. Through experimentation and reference tutorials, we identified TouchDesigner as the most suitable platform for creating the gesture based visuals and real time feedback system.

A major challenge was designing a full-body mirror display. While the initial idea was to use a single large monitor, we quickly realized this was not feasible. As a result, we explored alternatives such as projection with one way mirror film. Eventually, we discovered that the school had multiple stackable monitors available, which will allow us to construct a larger display behind the mirror surface instead of relying on a projector.

Moving forward, our goal is to develop a functioning gesture based smart mirror prototype for gym environments, refining both the interaction system and display setup to push the concept as far as possible within the scope of the project.
